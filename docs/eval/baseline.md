# Detection baseline

Generated by `scripts/eval.py` against the deterministic fixture set
(`scripts/make_fixtures.py`). The index is seeded with each scam
campaign's base image; scam variants are perturbed re-shares that should
still match, and clean images should not. A flag is any SCAM or AMBIGUOUS
verdict.

| Sensitivity | TP | FP | TN | FN | Precision | Recall | FPR |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| strict | 22 | 0 | 12 | 2 | 1.000 | 0.917 | 0.000 |
| balanced | 21 | 0 | 12 | 3 | 1.000 | 0.875 | 0.000 |
| permissive | 20 | 0 | 12 | 4 | 1.000 | 0.833 | 0.000 |

## Notes

- Precision and FPR are perfect across presets: no clean fixture is ever
  flagged, so the default ensemble weights and thresholds
  (`optimus.hashing.ensemble`) are kept as-is.
- Recall misses are heavy-crop re-shares (a border crop plus rescale
  shifts the low-frequency DCT enough to fall outside the band) and, at
  the permissive preset, an aggressive JPEG recompression. Loosening the
  thresholds to catch these would trade away the zero false-positive rate,
  which is the wrong trade for an auto-moderation action.
